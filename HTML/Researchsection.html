landing Page

We took the reference for our landing page from an organic food company, tessemaes.com The overall design looked simple,but informative. The page consists of our shop's logo and navigation bar aligned centrally. The image slider, created using JS,displays ongoing discount and other offers of our shop. Other links(such as search, cart etc)were not added in our page as it doesnt perform any function.

Why Us

During our research process, we found that lots of e-commerce website adds this section to highlight the benefits of purchasing items from thier company and build trust with the potentail customer. So, we refered the design from an indoor plant selling website thesill.com . Few changes were made from the original design such as alignment of items and an additoinal benefit. The spaces between the logo and text were also slightly adjusted.

Hover Effect

The hover and box shadow effect for our cards was inspired from a rental website: zillow.com and other e-commerce website such as : flipkart.com, floweraurara.com The effect looked very minimal yet elegant and subtle, which was just needed for our website. With just a simple CSS property( box-shadow, transform and transition), the effect was achieved. The border-radius, box-shadow and padding were adjusted slightly in our website.

Modal Popup

We found that popup modals are often used by many websites to encourage new users to subscribe thier newsletter, display ongoing discount and other information. So, by refering from thesill.com and other e-commerce website such as : lovehair.com,johnnycupcakes.com, we created a similar sign-up modal that popups after certain interval of time in the middle of the screen. Using JS, we also wanted to create a function by targetting the window object, so, a popup modal was created.

Article Overview

The design to display a brief overview of our article was inspired from an ecommerce plant shop,Bloomscape.com This section is created with flexbox and shows a brief introduction about the content of our main article and its title. The button is a link and navigates us to our main article page. The font-family and border-radius of button were changed to in our section to match with the main webpage.

Cutomer Review

The design from another indoor plant selling website, houseplantshop.com We believe this is an important section for any e-commerce website as it helps to build credibility and trust with the customers. Furthermore, we took this design as a challenge and as an opportunity to test our understanding of flexbox and box modal property of CSS.

Footer

Like in every other websites, our footer section is situated at the bottom of every webpage. We refered the design for our footer section from a solid perfume copmany,vivistix.com. Compared to other footer of websites that we researched, this looked much clean ,simple and organized. The section provides additonal links and information about shop and a functional sign up form. The layout was created simply with flexbox. Our section has a different background color compared to orignal design.
