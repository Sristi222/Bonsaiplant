FUKIEN TEA

Description

Fukien Bonsai plant is native to East Asia. This is one of the most popular bonsai plant among beginners. Starting from 15cm to 30cm, it grows up to 70 feet tall under ideal conditions. However this Fukien Outdoor Bonsai Plant is designed to grow at very low height to be kept inside the balcony for years to come. This outdoor bonsai plant is ideal for growing miniature bonsai plants in the town. Fukien Bonsai Plant is a slow growing plant, low maintenance and very less demanding plant and that makes it the best choice for beginners to start with.

THINGS TO KNOW

Watering

Fukien plant is suitable only for outdoors placed in full sunlight area. It can be kept indoors for a few days occasionally.

Location

Fukien is a water retaining plant and needs less watering. Plant can be watered once daily when placed outdoors or when the soil appears to be dry

Pruning

Prune timely to maintain the shape of the plant. Hard pruning of Fukien is preferred only in summers.

Repotting

Repotting should be done when roots cover the whole pot or once in 1 to 2 years. A mixture of sand, soil, lava granules, coco husk and bio compost is always good

Fertilizing

Fukien is fast growing plant with delicate roots and branches. It can be fertilized with NPK 19 19 19 or 20 20 20 fertilizer fortnightly.
