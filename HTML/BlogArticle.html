Influence of Technology in Bonsai Industry

Technology has played a crucial role in the substantial rise of the indoor plant market in recent years. Technology has transformed the way indoor plants are grown, sold, and cared for, but bought some negative consequence along with it.

12th December, 2022

TECHNOLOGY

Technology has had a big impact on every industry, revolutionising business operation and increasing production and efficiency. Technology has created new opportunities and addressed issues faced by conventional industries, from automation to data analysis.

As individuals explore for methods to add more greenery to their homes and workplaces, interest in indoor plants has grown over the past few years. Technology has contributed to this trend by offering creative approaches to indoor plant growth and management. As an illustration, consider intelligent planters that keep an eye on soil moisture, light, and temperature, or hydroponic systems that automate plant nutrition and offer a regulated growing environment. Because of these technical developments, caring for indoor plants is now simpler and more widely available, which has helped the indoor plant industry expand. Lets learn more about the impact of technology in bonsai business.

POSITIVE IMPACT

The indoor plant sector, among many others, has been profoundly influenced by the advancement of technology. Technology has greatly contributed to the expansion and popularity of this sector of the economy in recent years. It plays a positive role by making them easier to purchase, and simpler to cultivate. The bonsai plant business has improved in effectiveness, accessibility, and popularity because of cutting-edge technologies and methods available in the market. The benefits to the bonsai plant business are anticipated to grow and spread as technology develops, resulting in an even wider appreciation for these stunning and lovely plants.

Greater accessibility

Social media platform such as instagram, facebook, tictok have enabled seller to sell their plants to wider audience and plant enthusiasts to share their purchase. This has allowed more people to become aware and discover more about such beautiful plants.

Convient and time saving

Customers no longer have to travel to physical stores to purchase bonsai plants, and can instead place orders from the comfort of their own homes. Online shops offer fast and efficient delivery services, which means customers receive their bonsai plants quickly with minimal hassle. This has made the process of acquiring bonsai plants much easier and has removed many of the barriers that existed in the past.

Cost Reduction

The use of automated system has reduced the manual labor and minimized human error, leading to lower operational cost and increased productivity. More plants are also produced in shorter period of time, making overall process cost-effective.

Improved health

Demand for these goods has risen as more people are becoming aware of the advantages of indoor plants, including better air quality, lowered stress levels, and higher mental focus. The awareness of this positive message to wider audience is thanks to technology.

NEGATIVE IMPACT

Despite the numerous positive effects of the bonsai industry such as providing a source of income for many individuals, increasing cultural awareness, and promoting the art of bonsai, the industry is not immune to negative consequences.

Synthetic & Artifical Plants

synthetic and artificial indoor plants have been produced due to advancement in technology. These plants were created to resemble actual indoor plants but are composed of plastic and other materials. However, they do not offer the same advantages as actual plants, such as enhancing air quality and lowering stress levels. Such plants are much cheap, requires less maintainance leading to drop in demand for actual indoor plants.

Hacking & Data Loss

Hacking has become a growing concern for every industry. Almost regularly, the news reports file stolen of another major by data thieves. In our field, hacking may result in the failure of crucial systems including automated irrigation systems, climate controls, and lighting systems. This may lead to the loss of factories and a decline in the industry's productivity and profitability.

Crowded Space

The market for indoor plants is getting more congested, making it difficult for companies to stand out and compete. Everyone use the same technology which has made the business more crowded and people are being more aware about the business. This may result in price wars and a drop in profitability and quality of indoor plants.

Mechanical Disadvantage

We use different type of technology to preserve and maintain the condition of bonsai plant. These systems are prone to malfunciton and breakdown. So,if any of the machine or technology fail to give its output, the business faces numerous problem.

Bonsai Sales

The sale of a company is an important aspect which determines its success and growth. More clients can be drawn in, brand recognition can rise, and industry growth can be sparked by good sales success.

In recent years, technology has played a tremendous role on the sale of indoor plants. With the development of e-commerce platforms, users can now easily buy plants online. This has increased accessibility and convenience for purchasing indoor plants, which has directly increased sales. The expansion of indoor plant sales has also been aided by the usage of social media platforms. On social media sites like Instagram and Pinterest, many plant lovers have begun to share their passion for indoor plants, encouraging others buy and awaring other potential customer.This, combined with an increasing interest in sustainable living, has resulted in a significant increase in sales for the indoor plant industry. Other various factors of technology contributed to the sales of bonsai plant.

1) Digital Marketing

To reach potential consumer in past, indoor plant businesses depended on conventional techniques like print marketing. However, these businesses have been able to reach a considerably wider audience thanks to Google Ads, SEO, influencer marketing, email marketing etc

2) Advanced Automation

Automation has made it possible for customers to order our product, without the need for human interaction. This has increased the convenience for customers and has made it easier for them to purchase plants. It can track inventory levels and automatically reorder plants when stock is running low. It can handle frequently asked questions and provide quick responses to customers. This allows businesses to provide excellent customer service without the need for staff.

3)Online Marketplace

Plant lovers and gardeners now have a platform to purchase and sell a range of indoor plants thanks to the internet market. \ To buy indoor plants, customers are no longer need to physically visit a garden shop or a nursery. They can peruse a variety of plants from the convenience of their homes. Moreover,plant vendors can have the capacity to sell to people all over the world. This not only boosts business sales but also promotes the culutre of indoor gardening.

In conclusion, technology has both positive and negative impacts on the indoor plant marke. On the one hand, technology has allowed online seller to sell their plants and customer to purhcase with convenience. Additionally, technology has made it simpler for producers to automate their processes, boosting productivity and cutting expenses. But technology has also increased competition and encouraged in the production of fake and artifical plants.

While technology has undoubtedly had an influence on the indoor plant market and sales in general,it is important to conisder both positive and negative effects and make a wise judgement regarding the useage of technology.
